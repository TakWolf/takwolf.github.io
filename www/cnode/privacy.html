In addition to the aforementioned policy, the following information pertains to data collection, handling, storage, and sharing by the Chrome/Edge extension and cnodejs.org web service.

Data collection
  1. If you choose to save your chats on cnodejs.org via CNodeJS, your chats will be saved on cnodejs.org servers in Amazon US data center.
  2. Your registration data will also be saved on cnodejs.org servers in Amazon US data center.
  3. Your browser version may be saved and used to diagnose problems you experienced.
  4. If you choose to upload and save files, such as HTML and PDF files on cnodejs.org website, those data will be saved on cnodejs.org servers on Amazon US.
  5. We may use tools like Google analytics to collect usage data.
Data handling
  1. Data is transferred to servers through HTTPS.
  2. The company has strict restrictions on your data. Basically, no one will be able to access your data unless you request us to diagnose data related issues for you.
  3. We may get stats information of your data, for example, how many new chats the service has today etc.
  4. We may use third party services like Google Analytics to collect some usage data.
  5. We will not sell your data to any third party.
  6. You can choose to delete your account and remove all data stored in our servers.
Data Storage
  1. The data is stored in Amazon servers based in the US.
  2. We use a backup system in Amazon to back up the data.
  3. We utilize security services, such as firewalls, to safeguard your data.
Data sharing:
  1. We may use some third party services, such as Google analytics to collect some usage data, so some usage data is shared to those services.
  2. You can choose to share your chats with AI to other users.
  3. If you choose to Chat with AIs through our tools or services, you agree to share those data with AIs and the organization behind those AIs.
  4. Your chat data is not shared with any other third parties, except when required by law enforcement.
